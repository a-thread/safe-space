SAFE SPACE

about us

Members of the LGBTQ communtiy have long faced social stigma, discrimination and emotional challenges. Chances are, you or someone you know is currently enduring these hardships - has felt unwelcome in a restaurant, experienced sideways glances at a hotel, or struggled with basic community access. They may not know where or how to reach out to others in their area. Safe Space is here to answer that call. Rather than scouring the web for businesses and social media groups, you can sign up for your FREE Safe Space account and have these resources at your fingertips. Find LGBTQ-friendly restaurants, hair salons, community resources, and even connect on video chat with others. Or add a business to our growing list. So, what are you waiting for?

Join us and help make the LGBTQ community a Safe Space!
